# Dashboard_Blinkit_Analysis_PowerBI
An interactive Blinkit sales dashboard built in Excel to analyze $1M+ revenue, outlet performance, and customer trends. Provides actionable insights on sales distribution, item categories, and operational efficiency.



##  Overview

This project presents a comprehensive sales dashboard inspired by Blinkit (India's last-minute delivery app). The goal was to transform raw retail data into meaningful insights that help understand sales performance, customer behavior, and outlet efficiency.

The dashboard highlights key metrics such as total sales, average sales, number of items sold, and customer ratings—making it easier to identify trends and business opportunities.

---

##  Key Objectives

* Analyze overall sales performance and revenue distribution
* Compare outlet performance across different tiers and sizes
* Identify top-performing product categories
* Evaluate customer satisfaction using average ratings
* Enable data-driven decision-making through visual insights

---

##  Dashboard Highlights

* 💰 **Total Sales:** $1M+ revenue generated
* 📦 **Total Items Sold:** 8,523 items
* 📊 **Average Sales per Item:** $141
* ⭐ **Average Rating:** 3.92

---

##  Key Insights

* Supermarket Type 1 contributes the highest share of total sales (~$787K)
* Tier 3 locations generate the most revenue compared to Tier 1 and Tier 2
* Medium-sized outlets outperform small and high-sized outlets in sales
* Categories like snacks, dairy, and frozen foods show consistent demand
* Grocery stores have higher item visibility but lower total sales compared to supermarkets
